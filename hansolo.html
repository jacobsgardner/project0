Han Solo

Han Solo is Chewbaccs's partner and best friend. Chewbacca and Han Solo met during a time Chewie was imprisoned by imperial forces when Han was tossed into a pit Chewie was kept in for Chewie to kill him. Teaming up they escaped together and joined a crew of ciminals and took part in an attempted robery of a shipment of fuel being transported on a train. During this mission and the adventure that resulted from the subsequent fallout Chewie and Han became close friends. They remained friends and partners for over 30 years until Han's death at the hands of his son, the moody Kylo Ren.
