The Rebellion

IV V VI

IV

Chewie's path towards joining the rebellion began on the planet Tatooine, where he was hiding out with his partner, Han Solo, from the intergalactic ganster Jabba the Hutt, of the Hutt crime family. Han and Chewbacca were hired to transport the young Luke Skywalker and the ageing Obi-Wan (Old Ben) Kenobi to the planet Alderan. Upon discovering that Alderan was destroyed the passengers of the Milenium Falcon were captured by the Death Star, a small moon sized imperial ship and weapon. On the Death Star Chewie, posing as a hardened criminal, took part in the daring rescue of Princess Leaia Organa. Once the princess was returned to the Rebel Alliance Chewie and Han decided to take their reward and and leave to repay their debt to Jabba. But after having second thoughts they returned and helped helped the Rebel Alliance destroy the Death Star.

V

Chewbacca took part in the Rebel Alliance's defeat on the planet Hoth, were he was able to perform miracolous repairs on the Milenium Falcon allowing him and Han to escape from the clutches of the invading Imperial forces. While hiding out in Cloud City Chewbacca, along with his friends, were betrayed by Lando Calrisian. They then had to escape th imperial forces on the city, but during so lost Han Solo to the bounty hunter Boba Fett.

VI

Chewbacca then took part in the rescue of his best friend Han Solo from Jabba the Hutt. Once Solo was once again safe, him and Chewie teamed up for the incredibly important mission of destroying the shield generator for the Death Star. Teaming up with the Ewoks of Endor he, along with his team were able to defeat the imperial forces guarding the shield generators. This allowed the Rebel Alliance to once again destroy the Death Star, and this time defeat the Galactic Empire.
